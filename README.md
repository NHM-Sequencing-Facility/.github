

This GitHub organisation contains pipelines, tools, documentation and SOPs for the [Molecular Biology Laboratories](https://www.nhm.ac.uk/our-science/services/facilities/molecular-laboratories.html) at The Natural History Museum (NHM) in London. 

The laboratory facilities are available to Museum staff and associates, and training can be provided. Staffed by specialists in molecular biology, we carry out interdisciplinary research for all Museum departments. We also provide a bespoke sequencing service, from extraction through to sequence analysis. ​We offer support for molecular diagnostics and characterisation of a wide variety of sample types and sources. Leveraging our extensive experience, we routinely and successfully extract and sequence DNA or RNA from the most difficult of samples. We offer a wide range of molecular services, from training and public engagement through to rapid commercial diagnostics, both clinical and environmental.
